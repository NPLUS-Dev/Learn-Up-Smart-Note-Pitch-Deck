08 · Context-Aware AI Tutor

AI that's
reading your notes

  • 📄
    Full context, understood

    Profile (age, education, goals, occupation) + learning history + the entire current note — a pasted URL is crawled instantly. One shortcut.

  • 🔗
    Connects and builds on its own

    Surfaces links to prior concepts ("τ=Iα relates to the vector cross product you learned earlier!"), and when you're stuck generates equations, 3D models, and simulations for the a-ha moment.

  • 🧭
    When you don't know what you don't know

    Draws on a database of other learners' questions to ask the right one and let you find the answer yourself — and drafts a weekly study curriculum, added to your planner on approval.

＋ No pure-Socratic mode that only fires back questions — it breaks note-taking flow. Instead: a warm, clear, direct-explanation mode.
